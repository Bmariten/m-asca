Loans Details

Explore our loan options and use our calculators to plan your finances.

Ordinary Loan

Borrow up to 3x your savings at 1% interest with guarantors. Maximum repayment period: 36 months.

Temporary Loan

Borrow up to 2/3 of your savings at 1% interest. Maximum repayment period: 12 months.

Instant Loan

Borrow up to half your savings or Ksh 10,000 (whichever is lower) at 5% monthly interest. Repayment period: 1 month.

Chama Loan

Borrow up to 3x your savings at 2.5% interest. Maximum repayment period: 2 years.

Group Loan

Borrow up to 3x your savings at 2.5% interest. Maximum repayment period: 2 years.
