Study Tours

In partnership with Mama Promotions Women group (www.mamapromotions.com), we organize agricultural-based study tours for our members to different parts of the country as well as outside the country. The farm tours enable our members to see first-hand cutting-edge agricultural practices, giving them the opportunity to ask as many questions as they may feel necessary and henceforth making it easier for them to implement such practices on their farms with precision.

Expos

In partnership with Mama Promotions Women group, we organize county-based agribusiness expos where our members showcase their products, meet clients, and other stakeholders in the industry. We converge relevant resource persons in government and industry to share insights on issues of concern through panel discussions and presentations. We also provide opportunities for small and medium enterprises in the localities to exhibit their products and meet clients and important networks for their varied businesses.
