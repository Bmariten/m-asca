Understanding M-ASCA Model

M-ASCA is a virtual microfinance registered as a SACCO that uses the m-asca platform for all operations.

Key Features

Target Audience

Targets the unbanked and lesser off in society who may not have collateral for loans but have friends in different parts of the country.

Affordable Interest Rates

Interest rates are as low as 1%.

Paperless & Virtual

No wasting time in meetings and signing.

Convenient Savings

Convenient saving for low-income earners in outlets near their sources of income.

Flexible Savings

Savings of amounts as low as Ksh 50 anytime and as many times as one wishes.

Loan Repayment

Loan repayment in instalments provided that the whole instalment is in by the month.

Platform Features & The Difference

USSD & App

Platform on both USSD for low-end phones and an app for smartphones.

Simplified Menu

Simplified menu that is easy to navigate.

Inbuilt Features

No paybill numbers but a seamless process.

Open to All Kenyans

Open to all Kenyans of adult age.

Anytime, Anywhere

Mobile-based transactions anytime, anywhere.

Paperless Association

No meetings, no groups of 10-15, no fines for late coming or absenteeism.

Mobile Money Platform Procedures

My Account

Under this Module one can:

  • Change Pin
  • Configure Bank Account details
  • Change Next of Kin Details
  • Close Account

Enquiries

Dial *645*86# enter your m-asca pin, choose enquiries.

Check balances and statements of all savings and temporary accounts

Registration

Dial *645*86# and follow instructions step by step

To pay registration fee:

  • Enter safaricom service pin for m-pesa
  • Equitel pin for equitel

Savings

Available Savings Accounts:

  • Loan savings account
  • Temporary savings account
  • School fees account
  • Savings account

Payments:

Members directly save from:

  • M-pesa account using safaricom service pin
  • Equitel account using equitel pin

Loans

Available Loan Types:

  • Ordinary loan: 3x savings at 1% interest
  • Temporary loan: 2/3 of savings at 1% interest

Loan Limits:

  • Instant: 1 month max
  • Ordinary: 2,000-50,000 (6m), 51,000-100,000 (12m)

Send to Mpesa

Used to withdraw money from the temporary account to the m-pesa account

  1. Dial *645*86# and enter your m-asca pin
  2. Under the send to m-pesa sub menu enter the amount and submit
  3. You will receive an SMS notification from M-ASCA
  4. Use normal m-pesa withdrawal or transfer options once funds are received
