Business Opportunities

We do country-based research to identify business opportunities that our members can engage in and make good returns for their investment. We avoid encouraging our members to invest in saturated business activities where returns are marginal. Some of the activities we have introduced our members to include:

  • Poultry farming
  • Fish farming
  • Open irrigation
  • Hydroponic fodder
  • Onion farming among others
  • Urban horticulture
