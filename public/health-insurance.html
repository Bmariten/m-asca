Health Insurance

We are cognizant of the fact that if we are in the business of eliminating poverty, then households have to be free of illnesses. We partner with insurance firms providing fairly priced health insurance policies to ensure that illnesses are arrested early in advance to avoid depletion of family resources.

Most of our members are in business. We have partnered with Primelink Insurance Agency to provide underwriting of various policies on behalf of our clients. Members will be able to submit payments through the system little by little until full premiums are paid. Meanwhile, Primelink will be able to provide insurance cover for the clients from insurance companies of their choice. We are currently facilitating the following for our members:

  • Motorcycle insurance
  • Health insurance
  • Motor vehicle insurance
  • Life insurance
