Who We Are

M-ASCA is dedicated to empowering the underserved populations of Kenya through financial inclusion and dignity. Our virtual platform enables access to savings and credit, empowering individuals and small businesses to thrive.

Who We Are

M-ASCA is a virtual Microfinance model that allows members to accumulate savings and borrow up to three times their savings using social networks as guarantors. Accessible nationwide, M-ASCA provides a flexible, inclusive platform for all Kenyans.

Our Structure

The M-ASCA platform operates as a virtual SACCO, created by Mama Promotions Women Group and managed by professionals in the field. The system is maintained by Tangazoletu Ltd, an ICT company responsible for innovations such as Lipa Na M-Pesa.

Key Contributors

The M-ASCA model is the brainchild of Dr. Martina Mutheu Mulwa, a lecturer at the University of Nairobi with over five years of expertise in mobile money. The day-to-day operations are managed by members of Mama Promotions Women Group and supported by a team of professionals across various sectors.

Understanding the M-ASCA Model

M-ASCA is a virtual Microfinance model that allows members to accumulate savings and borrow up to three times their savings using social networks as guarantors. Accessible nationwide, M-ASCA provides a flexible, inclusive platform for all Kenyans.

Key Features

  • Open to all Kenyans of adult age
  • Mobile phone-based registration
  • Anytime, anywhere transactions
  • Paperless and eco-friendly
  • Flexible savings and borrowing options
  • Use of social networks as guarantors
  • Accessible via Equitel, Safaricom, and USSD

M-ASCA's Aim

Our aim is to dignify the Base of the Pyramid populations through a credit and loan association that unleashes the potential of members through active participation. We provide the freedom and transparency needed for financial empowerment.

"Financial Inclusion with Dignity"

Security of the Money

  • Registered as a SACCO and regulated
  • Real-time system updates for transparency
  • Operates with Safaricom Paybill and Equitel m-banking infrastructure
